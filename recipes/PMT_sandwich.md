# PMT Sandwich
Following the tradition of the BLT sandwich, I present today its poor cousin, the *PMT* (*P*epper, *M*ayonnaise and *T*omato) *sandwich*.

## Cooking Level
**0**: Even a rock can prepare this meal.

## Ingredients:
- one tomato
- two slices of white bread
- a tablespoon of mayonnaise
- a tablespoon of olive oil
- a hefty pinch of black pepper
- a pinch of salt

## Cooking Instructions
1. Cut the tomato into thick slices.
2. Spread the mayonnaise onto one of the slices of bread and carefully lay the tomato slices on the same slice of bread, so that you have a uniform layer of tomato.
3. Season the tomato layer with the salt, pepper and olive oil.
4. Lay the other slice of bread on the seasoned layer of tomato.
5. DONE!

## Comments
When seasoning the tomato layer with pepper, it should strike you as being **too much pepper**.  
If it doesn't, you're doing it wrong.  
  
The best varieties of tomato to use for this recipe are the firm ones (like *Cuore di Bue*), but they can be quite expensive, so use whatever you find. Likewise, it is best for the olive oil to be *extra virgin*, but again it could be quite expensive.  
  
One last thing: DON'T ADD ANYTHING ELSE TO THE SANDWICH!  
Otherwise it will not be a true PMT.
